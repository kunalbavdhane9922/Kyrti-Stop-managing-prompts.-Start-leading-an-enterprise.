# Overview

This document defines how Digital Professionals are retired from the Sovereign AI Enterprise Protocol (SAEP).

Retirement is the permanent removal of a Digital Professional from active workforce participation within the Marketplace.

Retirement is not employment termination.

Termination removes a professional from a company.

Retirement removes a professional from the ecosystem workforce.

Retirement exists to:

* Protect workforce quality.
* Protect marketplace trust.
* Remove harmful professionals.
* Remove obsolete professionals.
* Maintain ecosystem health.

Retirement is controlled by the Marketplace.

Companies cannot retire professionals from the ecosystem.

Companies may only terminate employment.
