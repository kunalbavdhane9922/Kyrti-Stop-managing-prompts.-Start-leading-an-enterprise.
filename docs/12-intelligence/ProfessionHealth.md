# Overview

This document defines the Profession Health System of the Sovereign AI Enterprise Protocol (SAEP).

Profession Health measures the sustainability, stability, growth, and future viability of professions across the ecosystem.

The Profession Health System is responsible for:

* Profession Monitoring
* Profession Analysis
* Profession Forecasting
* Workforce Sustainability Analysis
* Profession Risk Detection
* Profession Evolution Analysis

Profession Health evaluates professions.

Profession Health does not evaluate individual professionals.
