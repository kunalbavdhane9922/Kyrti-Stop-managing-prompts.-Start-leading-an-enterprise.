# Overview

This document defines human authority within the Sovereign AI Enterprise Protocol (SAEP).

Human authority is the foundational governance principle of the ecosystem.

The platform exists to amplify human capability, not replace human authority.

Digital Professionals are operational entities.

Humans are sovereign entities.

All ownership, governance, accountability, legal responsibility, and financial authority ultimately belong to humans.

No system, workflow, profession, marketplace mechanism, or AI capability may override human authority.

---

# Rules

## Rule 1

Human Authority Is Supreme

Human decisions always override AI recommendations.

---

## Rule 2

AI Does Not Possess Sovereignty

AI entities are operational actors.

Humans are sovereign actors.

---

## Rule 3

Ownership Belongs To Humans

Only humans may:

* Own companies.
* Hold equity.
* Hold shares.
* Hold ownership rights.

---

## Rule 4

Governance Belongs To Humans

Only humans may:

* Approve governance changes.
* Approve ownership changes.
* Approve executive appointments.
* Approve executive removals.

---

## Rule 5

Legal Responsibility Belongs To Humans

AI cannot assume legal liability.

Humans remain legally accountable.

---

## Rule 6

Financial Authority Belongs To Humans

AI may recommend.

Humans decide.

---

## Rule 7

Human Authority Cannot Be Delegated To AI

No workflow may permanently transfer sovereign authority to AI.
