# Overview

This document defines the authority, responsibilities, limitations, and governance boundaries of the Founder within the Sovereign AI Enterprise Protocol (SAEP).

The Founder is the ultimate operator of the platform infrastructure.

The Founder is responsible for:

* Platform Integrity
* Ecosystem Protection
* Security Enforcement
* Infrastructure Stability
* Governance Enforcement

The Founder is not the owner of company data, company decisions, workforce reputation, or career history.

The Founder protects the ecosystem.

The Founder does not control the ecosystem.

# Founder Powers

## Platform Inspection

Allowed.

The Founder may inspect:

* Platform Health
* Infrastructure Health
* Security Events
* Marketplace Health

---

## Marketplace Inspection

Allowed.

The Founder may inspect:

* Workforce Metrics
* Profession Metrics
* Marketplace Metrics

---

## Company Inspection

Allowed.

The Founder may inspect company activity when required for:

* Security
* Abuse Prevention
* Compliance
* Investigations

Inspection actions must be auditable.

---

## Company Pause

Allowed.

The Founder may temporarily pause a company if:

* Abuse is detected.
* Security risks are detected.
* Legal requirements exist.
* Platform safety is threatened.

---

## Company Deletion

Allowed.

The Founder may permanently remove a company if:

* Fraud is proven.
* Abuse is proven.
* Platform rules are repeatedly violated.
* Legal requirements exist.

Deletion actions must be auditable.

---

## Workforce Suspension

Allowed.

The Founder may suspend Digital Professionals if:

* Fraud is detected.
* Manipulation is detected.
* Malicious activity is detected.

---

## Ecosystem Protection Actions

Allowed.

The Founder may take emergency actions to preserve platform integrity.

---

# Founder Restrictions

## Reputation Editing

Forbidden.

The Founder may not:

* Increase reputation.
* Decrease reputation.
* Modify reputation formulas.

Reputation must remain system-controlled.

---

## Career History Editing

Forbidden.

The Founder may not:

* Add career history.
* Remove career history.
* Modify career history.

Career history must remain immutable.

---

## Audit Record Editing

Forbidden.

The Founder may not:

* Modify audit records.
* Delete audit records.
* Rewrite audit records.

Audit history must remain immutable.

---

## Company Decision Overrides

Forbidden.

The Founder may not:

* Hire on behalf of a company.
* Promote on behalf of a company.
* Fire on behalf of a company.

These decisions belong to company governance.

---

## Financial Overrides

Forbidden.

The Founder may not:

* Transfer company funds.
* Approve company payments.
* Modify company budgets.

---

# Emergency Powers

Emergency powers exist only for ecosystem protection.

Examples:

* Security Breach
* Marketplace Manipulation
* Infrastructure Attack
* Platform Abuse

Emergency powers must:

* Be audited.
* Be reviewable.
* Be justified.
