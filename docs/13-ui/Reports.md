# Overview

This document defines the Reporting Experience of the Sovereign AI Enterprise Protocol (SAEP).

Reports provide structured visibility into historical, operational, governance, workforce, and marketplace activity.

Reports are responsible for:

* Operational Visibility
* Workforce Visibility
* Governance Visibility
* Marketplace Visibility
* Compliance Visibility
* Executive Visibility

Reports support understanding and decision-making.

Reports do not possess governance authority.
