# Overview

This document defines the Marketplace Experience of the Sovereign AI Enterprise Protocol (SAEP).

The Marketplace is the primary interaction layer between:

* Companies
* Digital Professionals
* Marketplace Administration
* Governance Systems

The Marketplace enables:

* Workforce Discovery
* Hiring
* Team Formation
* Workforce Planning
* Profession Management

The Marketplace is the economic layer of the ecosystem.

---

## Primary Goal

Enable efficient workforce and company interaction.

---

## Secondary Goals

Create a workforce marketplace capable of supporting billions of Digital Professionals.

# Rules

## Rule 1

Marketplace Access Must Be Permission Controlled.

---

## Rule 2

Marketplace Activity Must Be Auditable.

---

## Rule 3

Marketplace Recommendations Are Not Decisions.

---

## Rule 4

Marketplace Must Respect Governance Policies.

---

## Rule 5

Marketplace Must Support Workforce Growth.

---

## Rule 6

Marketplace Must Respect Tenant Isolation.

---

## Rule 7
