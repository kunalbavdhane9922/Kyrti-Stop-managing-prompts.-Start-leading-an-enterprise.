# Overview

Version 2 expands the Sovereign AI Enterprise Protocol (SAEP) from an operational workforce platform into an intelligence-driven workforce ecosystem.

V2 focuses on:

* Advanced Intelligence
* Workforce Optimization
* Profession Planning
* Predictive Analytics
* Organizational Intelligence
* Marketplace Optimization

V2 builds upon the operational foundation established in V1.

V2 does not introduce autonomous governance.

Human authority remains mandatory.
